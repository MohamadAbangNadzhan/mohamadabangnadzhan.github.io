This website is for people that are bored. According to this Wikipedia page, boredom, also known as ennui, or tedium, is an emotion characterised by uninterest in one's surrounding, often caused by a lack of distractions or occupations. It seems to be a specific mental state that people find unpleasant - a lack of stimulation that leaves them craving relief, with a host of behavioral, medical and social consequences.

This website aims not to cure your boredom, in fact, this website welcomes boredom and the author intended for the visitor of this website to be so bored when they checked it out that they started to wonder to themselves on what they are doing on this site at the first place.
